How to Cook My Favorite Meal

Egusi Soup


Ingredients

  • cup of grounded egusi (melon seeds)
  • 500g assorted meat
  • ponmo (cow skin)
  • Stockfish
  • Crayfish
  • Palm oil
  • Seasoning cubes
  • Salt and pepper
  • Ugu leaves

Steps

  1. Wash and boil the meat, ponmo, and stockfish with seasoning cubes and onions.
  2. Heat palm oil in the pot, add blended egusi and stir continously til it thickens.
  3. Pour in the cooked meat and stock into the egusi mix
  4. Add crayfish, pepper, and more seasoning if needed.
  5. Let it cook for 10-15 minutes on low heat.
  6. Add your washed ugu leaves and simmer for a few minutes.
  7. Stir and taste. Adjust seasoning. Turn off the heat.

Summary

egusi soup is one of the mosy popula soupa in Nigeria. It's thick, rich, and goes well with pounded yam, semo, or eba(my favorite).

The flavor depends heavily on the palm oil, meat stock, and how wel you fry the egusi. Don't rush the frying step.

Once you get the base right, every step is straightforward. It's filling and can be stored and can be store in the freezer for later.
