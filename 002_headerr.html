Lagos Morning

The danfo horns were already blaring by 6am. Tolu dashed out, trying not to miss his bus to Yaba Tech.

The Wallet

At Oshodi, he reached for his wallet. Gone. Panic. He had just withdrawn N50000 for school fees.

UNexpected Help

An old woman selling boli noticed. "Na your wallet be this?" she asked, waving it with a smile.

A Small Crowd

People gathered. "Na good thing to return am," someone said. Lagos wasn't all madness, after all.

Lesson of the Day

Tolu thanked her, bought two boki, and gave her N500. she refude. He insisted. She smiled.

Moving On

As the danfo sped off, Tolu stared out the window, thinking: Lagos has problems, but Lagos has heart.
